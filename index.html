Meet the Google Photos Panel for Photoshop

The Google Photos Panel for Photoshop simplifies your access to Google Photos while working in Adobe Photoshop by giving you easy access to all the images you have synced to Google Photos. You can manage your entire photo collection from withing this small panel, and open your photos in Photoshop without ever leaving Photoshop itself.

Features

Photos

All your Google Photos

can easily be viewed and managed within Photoshop.

Albums

You can easily create and edit albums within the panel.

Assistant

The AI-Powered assistant is also available in the panel to suggest new ways to display your photos!
